As the world is developing, more and more people are consuming meat. The production of meat is a big strain on the environment due to methane gas, animal food production, wateruse, and transport. But also because other trees, crops, and fields are being destroyed to make way for animal farming. In other words, meat production has a high carbon footprint.

As this knowledge is being more widely shared, more people are looking into ways to reduce their carbon footprint . One way is to change the way we produce and consume protein. Protein is a key building block for a healthy lifestyle, but protein need to come from meat alone.

This website will allow you to explore alternative protein sources that can help you reduce your carbon footprint!
